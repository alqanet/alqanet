## 👋 About

**ALQANET** is a manpower & recruitment platform connecting companies in Qatar with skilled talent — built end-to-end by me, from database design to deployment.

- 🏢 Company registration & admin approval workflows
- 👨‍💼 Candidate profiles, CV uploads & smart matching
- 🔐 Secure auth (Email verification + Google login)
- 💳 Subscription / plan management for companies
